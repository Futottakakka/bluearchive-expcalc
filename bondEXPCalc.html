Sensei Account EXP Calculator

A simple calculator to show much relationship/bond EXP is needed to get from level X to level Y.

Will also show a table of the various gift types and how many of them it will take to level up their bond level.

The optimized path isn't super useful for large level gaps, but intended for something like level 20-22.

Even then, getting large numbers of 3* gifts is hard so, YMMV.
